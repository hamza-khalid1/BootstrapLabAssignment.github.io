My Services


Penetration Test

I provide expert penetration testing services to identify and address vulnerabilities in your network, applications, and infrastructure. Using real-world attack simulations, I assess areas such as network security, web applications, wireless networks, and mobile apps to pinpoint weaknesses like SQL injection, cross-site scripting, and misconfigurations. I also offer social engineering assessments to test human vulnerabilities. With a certified ethical hacking background, my approach ensures comprehensive, actionable reports tailored to your specific needs, helping you strengthen your defenses and safeguard sensitive data from potential threats.

Vulnerability Assessment

I offer comprehensive vulnerability assessment services to identify and evaluate security weaknesses in your systems, applications, and networks. Through automated tools and manual analysis, I scan for common vulnerabilities, such as outdated software, misconfigurations, and potential entry points for attackers. My services provide in-depth reports with detailed findings and actionable recommendations to remediate risks, helping you proactively secure your infrastructure and ensure compliance with industry standards, while minimizing the potential for exploitation by cybercriminals.

Incident Response and Forensics

I provide incident response services to help organizations quickly detect, contain, and recover from cyberattacks or security breaches. My approach includes identifying the source and scope of the attack, mitigating further damage, and restoring operations securely. In addition, I offer digital forensics services to investigate the incident thoroughly. This includes collecting and analyzing digital evidence, identifying attack methods, and preserving crucial data for legal or compliance purposes. My services ensure that your organization can respond effectively to incidents, recover from them, and prevent future threats.
